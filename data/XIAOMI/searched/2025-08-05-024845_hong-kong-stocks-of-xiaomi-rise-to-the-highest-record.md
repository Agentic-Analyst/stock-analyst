On January 4, 2025, the Hong Kong stock of Xiaomi Group (01810.HK) soared to a remarkable height. The company’s stock soared over 6.18%, standing at HK$36.1 in the latest session. It once peaked at HK$36.25 during the day, which was the highest since January 5, 2021. The total market value has reached over HK$900 billion, reflecting the confidence of investors.

Milestones in Xiaomi’s Stock Performance

Listing Journey: On July 9, 2018, Xiaomi listed on the main board of the Hong Kong Stock Exchange under the name “Xiaomi Group.” Reforms to Hong Kong’s listing rules led it to become the first company listed with a dual-class share structure.

Early Challenges : Despite the promising start, Xiaomi’s stock price fell below its issue price of HK$17 and eventually reached a low of HK$8.28 per share in September 2019 .
